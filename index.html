上課地點在哪裡？ / Where is the location? +
主要於 Markham 及 Richmond Hill 地區。具體健身房地點將於諮詢時確認。
Mainly in Markham and Richmond Hill. Gym details will be confirmed during consultation.
新手適合參加嗎？ / Is it suitable for beginners? +
絕對適合。我會由最基礎的動作控制與呼吸教起，確保新手也能安全起步。
Yes. We start with fundamental movement control and breathing to ensure a safe start for beginners.
是否提供飲食建議？ / Do you provide nutrition advice? +
是的。我會根據您的身體數據提供科學的宏觀營養建議，而非極端節食。
Yes. I provide scientific macro recommendations based on your body data, no extreme dieting.
需要訓練多久才見效？ / How long to see results? +
通常堅持 8-12 週會見到明顯身形變化，我們會注重長遠且可持續的轉變。
Noticeable changes typically appear in 4-8 weeks. We focus on long-term, sustainable results.
收費模式是怎樣的？ / What is the pricing model? +
我們提供靈活的課時套餐。詳細報價將在初步評估後提供。
